Advanced Programming: Final Project

Davide Bonfanti, Sofia Ognibene, Daniele Pozzobon

This web app allows the user to perform different analytical operations on the DisGeNET COVID-19 data collection. The dataset was obtained identifying mentions of diseases, signs and symptoms among a selection of papers referring to Coronavirus 19 disease. Data are collected in two different sub-datasets, one related to genes and one anoter related to diseases.
